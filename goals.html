Goals

The pyramids and the marketplace atmosphere, i would also like to go diving near egypt as i have seen friends photos of Sharm El-Sheikh and the amazing variety of sea life.

Ever since i was i child i have wanted to visit Niagara Falls, i have always loved waterfalls and the look of water and the way it interacts with light.

Louvre Paris

Paris has many tourist attractions, i definitely want to visit the effel tower and the nearby Grand palais but most of all i would walk around the Louvre.

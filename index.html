Welcome To My Site, Created For My University Project.
The site acts as a autobiography and allows me to share my experiences and hobbies.

The Website's Origin

Before i made this site i was originally looking for a source of inspiration, one of my main focuses was that i wanted to collect all my own resources for the project.

One evening i was sat in bella italia eating dinner and talking with my family members when i decided that the occasion would make a great photo, this sparked the idea for an autobiography site.

I already had many photos i could use as resources and i already owned the copyrights to the images or could obtain permission very easily, this was the start of the website.
